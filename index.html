SOUNDTRACK OF NOW

From the Stage to Now

We're working on pre-production of a documentary about local legends from the late '80s and '90s music scene — musicians who carried the passion of their band days into the rest of their lives, building meaningful careers, families, and communities in unexpected ways off the stage.

We're looking for former musicians who played in local bands between **1987 and 1999** — the era of **big energy, basements, garages, house parties, local bars**, and that unforgettable spark of "we're really doing this."

IF THIS SOUNDS LIKE YOU:

  • ★ Played in a local cover or original band during that time.
  • ★ Found your life has grown in ways you didn't predict back then... maybe you're teaching high school chemistry, found your path as a hospice nurse, bought a small farm in rural Ohio, or find your days filled with hosting tea parties with your kids.
  • ★ Still carry that era's spirit with you, even though your definition of success evolved in different ways.

We'd love to connect.

This film is a nostalgic celebration of how the same spark that electrified a garage at midnight can continue to shape a life in unexpected, meaningful ways.

If this sounds like you, please email us with the following details:

  • 1. The band/type of band, year(s), and city/state/country you played in.
  • 2. Brief description on what your life looks like today / how it looks different.
  • 3. Anything you'd like to share about what the transition/evolution has meant for you.
